# Axion Client Demo Card

## Run
```
bash scripts/demo_launch.sh
```

## Open
```
http://localhost:7777/dashboard
```

## First 5 clicks

1. **Look at the Overview card** (top of Portfolio tab)
2. **Click AAPL** in the holdings table
3. **Insights → News** → click a **Federal Reserve** news item
4. **Insights → Digest**
5. **Alerts tab** → switch filter to **Critical & High**

## What to say

1. "The overview monitors your portfolio continuously. It's flagging elevated risk — interest rates, geopolitical risk, and regulation are active signals classified from live news feeds. Fully auditable."

2. "Click any holding to see its risk profile. Apple has a critical rate-sensitivity alert and a known supplier dependency on TSMC from our relationship graph."

3. "Every event is automatically classified. This Fed event was tagged as an interest-rate signal. The explanation traces exactly what triggered the classification."

4. "The daily digest summarizes what matters — one critical alert, two high, five macro signals observed. The risk flag says: review before market open."

5. "Operators can filter alerts by severity. Each alert includes a suggested next step and links back to the source event."

## Do not click

- **Analysis Notes** tab (empty without AI)
- **Generate Fresh Digest** button (pre-built one is stronger)
- **PDF/image upload** (needs AI vision — CSV works)

## If asked about AI

"The core intelligence engine is deterministic and runs without AI — fully auditable. The AI layer adds conversational queries and deeper per-holding analysis. We connect that during deployment."
